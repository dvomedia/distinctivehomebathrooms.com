About Distinctive Home Bathrooms…  

Distinctive Home Bathrooms has been operating in and around Grantham, since 2006. Originally from Burton On Trent, DH bathrooms was registered as a UK Ltd company, DH Bathrooms Ltd in 2004. Distinctive Home Bathrooms employs a small but effective team of time served craftsmen. Working together, to complete all aspects of the bathroom installation.

We are a preferred installer of Saniflo showering enclosures and have teamed up with a major blue chip shower enclosure manufacturer, to offer the most fantastic same day services. Please click HERE to read more about SANIFLO and how we utilise their expertise to offer a brilliant service to you!

We like to work with discerning clients who appreciate craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Distinctive Home Bathrooms can source high quality, named brand products at large discounts meaning that the client receives a top class installation, for an amazing price.  

Through our experience, we provide a full comprehensive service. From the logistical planning stages to completion, the client can relax knowing that all stages will be planned, and executed perfectly.
