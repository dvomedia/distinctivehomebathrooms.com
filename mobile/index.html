Are you looking for a new bathroom, wet room, shower room or easy access bathroom?


Distinctive Home Bathrooms offer a range of services, to suit all needs.


Easy Access bathrooms for the less mobile, elderly and disabled have become a very popular choice over the last few years. We have created a large number of these rooms, making peoples every day life easier and more enjoyable.


Distinctive Home Bathrooms has been operating in and around Grantham, since 2006. Originally from Burton On Trent, DH bathrooms was registered as a UK Ltd company, DH Bathrooms Ltd in 2004. Distinctive Home Bathrooms employs a small but effective team of time served craftsmen. Working together, to complete all aspects of the bathroom installation.


We like to work with discerning clients who appreciate attention to detail and craftsmanship. Distinctive Home Bathrooms can source high quality, named brand products at large discounts meaning that the client receives a top class installation, for an amazing price.
