What is a mobility shower?


Many of us do and most of us will find it difficult to get in and out of a bath. This shouldn’t mean that bathing becomes a chore and cause of stress. A mobility shower enables you to enjoy bathing again. After all we spend over 1 ½ years doing it over our lifetime so why not make it pleasurable?


Access is easy and safe with an ultra-low tray threshold and secure hand bars. You can then stand or sit comfortably under a thermostatically controlled, safe and refreshing shower.
